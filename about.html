About Whole Body

Located in the beautiful blue ridge mountains, Whole Body Gym is a different kind of gym.

A better gym!

At Whole Body Gym, we take pride in what we do.

Our passion is helping people develop healthy relationships with their own bodies.

We know how easy it is to forget that a healthy body is the indispensable foundation of a healthy mind.

Whole Body Gym is here to help you remember!

With state of the art facilities and top notch instructors, Whole Body Gym is completely free for a limited time only!

Come on down to Whole Body Gym and we will get you fit and healthy once again.

If you're interested or have any questions, please contact us. We would love to hear from you!
